Open source Max Msp projects

This resource is developed by a community of Max developers to store, document, and code-version any projects/code that doesn't otherwise have a proper home. Ultimately the goal is to provide an easy-to-access directory of projects that are otherwise without a home.

Want to help? Right now it is little more than an unorganized stash of random patches from anywhere.
